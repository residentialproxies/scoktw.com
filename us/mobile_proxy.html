Which is the best between 3G, 4G, and 5G?

5G offers significantly faster speeds and lower latency, making it ideal for bandwidth-intensive tasks like high-definition video streaming or online gaming. However, 5G networks in some regions may not be as widely available as 3G/4G networks. 5G technology tends to be more expensive regarding infrastructure and service plans. On the other hand, 3G/4G proxies provide broader geographic coverage and are often more cost-effective.

Can mobile proxies be replaced?

Mobile proxies are replaceable and can be changed by switching SIM cards or mobile devices. Providers may offer options to switch between IP addresses or network locations.

Why are mobile proxies expensive compared to other proxies?

Mobile proxies are pricier than other types due to several factors. They require significant investment in infrastructure, including hardware and software. Their superior reliability and performance, limited availability of mobile IP addresses, and compliance with legal regulations further drive up prices.
