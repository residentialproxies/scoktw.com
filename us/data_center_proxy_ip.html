What is a data center proxy?

A data center proxy is an IP assigned to a server through which your traffic is routed to change your IP address and location.

What are the advantages of data center proxy?

With shorter journeys and a simplified architecture, data center proxies are both the fastest type of proxy and the most cost-effective option.
The data center IP is static and can be reserved for as long as needed. This makes them ideal for use cases like account management, allowing you to work on multiple seller profiles or social media accounts at once without getting blocked.

Is the data center proxy fast?

Data center proxies are fully hosted in data centers and are not affiliated with Internet service providers. This simplified architecture makes them faster than ISPs or residential proxies because they require one less "hop" from the server hosting your IP.

How to choose a shared or dedicated data center proxy?

While dedicated data center proxies are best for managing multiple accounts or social media profiles, shared data center proxies have their advantages too.
